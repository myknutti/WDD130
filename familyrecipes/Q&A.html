Questions & Answers

  • Is it hard to post a recipe?
  • Not at all! All you do is click a button and enter in the recipe!
  • Why come visit my site?
  • Families will come to my site because it is filled with childhood recipes that can bring you comfort when you are feeling down. I know when I am having a rough day at college and want a piece of home I go and recreate a recipe from my childhood. Then just for that moment, I am back at home and in the comfort of my family. Life can be difficult sometimes and to have a taste of home where you can feel love is all that matters.
  • What will you get when you visit my site?
  • Visitors will come to my site instead of someone else’s because they can have the ability to create their own. It will be set up similar to an Instagram page where you can add, follow, like, and save all your favorite recipes and you can have that be a connection to your family. You can customize it any way you want. Super simple and easy.
  • Questions answered
  • This site answers the question of when you don’t know what to bake you can find the recipes from your old favorites. Or when your family back home isn’t picking up you can have the recipes right there with all the correct measurements and all the correct ingredients. No more guessing because no one at home will pick up. So it answers the question of what to eat and how to make it.
